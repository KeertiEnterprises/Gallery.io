Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom curtains, window blinds, and installation process.

Do you provide free home measurement visits in Basti?
×

Yes, absolutely! We offer a 100% Free, no-obligation Home Visit across Basti city and nearby areas. Our window decor expert will bring physical fabric shade cards, measure your windows with precision laser tools, and give you an exact price quote on the spot.

What types of blinds do you customize?
+

We specialize in a comprehensive range: Roller Blinds (Sunscreen & Blackout), Zebra Blinds (Day & Night translucent stripes), Venetian Wooden & Aluminum Blinds, Motorized Remote-Controlled Blinds, and Roman Shades engineered with heavy durability controllers.

How long does it take for manufacturing and installation?
+

Once you select and confirm your design fabric, standard custom order production, stitching, and professional installation are completed within 3 to 5 business days. For urgent or commercial hotel requirements, we also offer express setup!

Are your curtain fabrics machine washable?
+

Yes! Most of our designer jacquard, sheer, and cotton-silk blend curtains are engineered for easy home maintenance and gentle machine washing. For heavy velvet or thermal blackout draperies, we guide you on proper dry-cleaning maintenance.
